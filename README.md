About This Assignment
This is a research-based glossary assignment covering 38 essential terms used across Business Analytics. The terms span four sections — Foundational Concepts, Data and Measurement, Customer and Sales KPIs, and Marketing and Campaign Analytics.
Every definition was written in my own words after researching each term from credible academic and industry sources. The assignment includes a real-world example for every term, supporting tables and illustrations, and a full reference list of 31 sources.
